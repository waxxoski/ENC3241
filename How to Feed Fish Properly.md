# Step 1
Turn on the aquarium light allowing you to see the fish and through the water clearly.

# Step 2
Open the fish food container by twisting or lifting the lid.

# Step 3
Gather the fish food into the lid. 
![[Fish food in tin.png]]
**Figure 1. Fish food in lid (Photo by Brian Nguyen)**
# Step 4
Sprinkle food bit by bit evenly across the surface. 

# Step 5
Push stuck food off decorations or plant using a long stick or implement.
![[Hidden away food.png|285]]
**Figure 2. Hidden away food (Photo by Brian Nguyen)**
# Step 6
Watch to make sure fish are interested in all food dropped.

**![[Cory eating.png|342]]
**Figure 3. Corydora Catfish eating food (Photo by Brian Nguyen)**
# Step 7
Store fish food container in a dry place.

# Step 8
Use a stick or a funnel to grab unfinished food.
