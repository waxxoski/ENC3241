# Instructions

[# Step 1
Turn on the aquarium light allowing you to see the fish and through the water clearly.

# Step 2
Open the fish food container by twisting or lifting the lid.

# Step 3
Pour the fish food into the lid. 
<p align="center">
  <img src="Fish food in tin.png" width="400"/>
</p>

<p align="center">
  Figure 1. Fish food in the lid (Photo by Brian Nguyen)
</p>

# Step 4
Sprinkle food evenly across the surface, bit by bit. 

# Step 5
Push stuck food off decorations or plants using a long stick or implement.
<p align="center">
  <img src="Hidden away food.png" width="400"/>
</p>

<p align="center">
  Figure 2. Hidden away food (Photo by Brian Nguyen)
  
# Step 6
Watch to make sure the fish are interested in all the food dropped.
<p align="center">
  <img src="Cory eating.png" width="400"/>
</p>

<p align="center">
  Figure 3. Corydora Catfish eating food (Photo by Brian Nguyen)
  
# Step 7
Store the fish food container in a dry place.

# Step 8
Use a net or a funnel to grab unfinished or untouched food.
